Our Story

What began as a quiet conversation over coffee in a Florentine bookshop became the adventure of a lifetime. Amara and Julian discovered in each other a rare and beautiful thing — a kindred spirit, a steady hand, a home. Two years, four continents, and one very patient dog later, Julian got down on one knee at sunrise on the Amalfi Coast, and the answer was written long before the question.
